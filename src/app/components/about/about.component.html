About and our Story

Welcome to LIGGÉEY, a Senegalese-born remote outsourcing company with a global vision. Founded in Amsterdam and expanded to Dakar, Senegal, we're on a mission to make African talent accessible to the Global market. We prioritize skills over diplomas, connecting businesses with a diverse pool of experts through remote job opportunities. Join us in redefining the future of work across borders.
We embraced the potential of a worldwide workforce. Now, our story is about connecting continents, breaking barriers, and making African talent accessible to Global businesses through remote opportunities.
Remote sounds exciting, but does not mean people work from home. We provide high-end workplaces to all our talent, with fast internet access to be sure they can be the highest value to the business.

About Us

Liggeey connects Africa's top talent with leading European companies for remote work opportunities. By harnessing the vast potential of skilled professionals across the African continent, we bridge the gap between talent and opportunity, offering businesses access to high-quality, diverse expertise while providing African professionals with global career growth.

What We Do

OUR MISSION

To empower African talent by creating global opportunities for remote work with European companies, fostering innovation, and promoting a more inclusive and diverse workforce.

OUR VISION

To become the leading platform that connects Africa's untapped talent with global businesses, transforming the future of work by making it more accessible, inclusive, and connected.

OUR WORK

We specialize in matching highly skilled professionals from Africa with top-tier European companies. Through our platform, we enable remote work opportunities in a wide range of industries, ensuring both professionals and companies thrive in a flexible, global environment.

Our Pillars

At Liggeey, our platform is built on four key pillars, all under the umbrella of Online Services:

  • Online Development: Empowering professionals with the tools and training they need for growth.
  • Online Support: Offering ongoing assistance to both talent and employers to ensure success.
  • Online Protection: Ensuring a secure and fair working environment for all parties.
  • Online Implementation: Providing seamless integration of remote work for businesses and professionals.

These pillars enable us to create a reliable and efficient platform for remote work opportunities.
